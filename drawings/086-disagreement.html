#086

The page divided by a vertical line through the centre. On the left half, parallel lines at forty-five degrees up-right, coloured in the cycle red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet from the bottom-left corner to the top-right. On the right half, parallel lines at forty-five degrees down-right, the same cycle running from the bottom-right corner to the top-left.

Seed — disagreement: a being at variance; dissimilitude; unlikeness.
